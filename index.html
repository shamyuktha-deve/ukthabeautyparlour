About Uktha

A Sanctuary of Beauty & Grace

Founded in 2012, Uktha Beauty Parlour was born from a passion for celebrating individuality through the art of beauty. We believe every person deserves to feel extraordinary, and our skilled team of beauty artists bring that vision to life through personalised, expert care.

Our parlour is more than just a salon — it's a haven where luxury meets self-care. From the moment you walk in, you're embraced by warmth, expertise, and the finest beauty products curated from around the world.
